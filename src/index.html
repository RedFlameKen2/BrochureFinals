Our Company

Century Properties Group, Inc. (“CPGI”) is one of the leading real estate companies in the Philippines, with over 38 years of experience. The Company is primarily engaged in the development, marketing, and sale of mid- and high-rise condominiums and single detached homes, leasing of retail and office space, and property management.

Mission

  • Hold fast to the principles of good design and proper construction, knowing that successful projects depend on their mastery and the application of best practices.
  • Work with the most trusted names in architectural design and luxury living to provide our clients with great properties of life-enhancing value.
  • Treasure talented individuals and the synergy they create when working as a solid, committed team.
  • Work with purpose, creating properties that surpass the needs of our clients, encourage communities, and enhance the surrounding landscape.
  • Strive for innovation, pursuing fresh ideas and architecture so that everything we make looks, feels, and behaves like nothing else on the market.
  • Adapt sustainable practices and find that preserving our planet for future generations is a fun and engaging part of our development philosophy.

Vision

Our vision is to be the Philippines’ foremost creator of innovative, well-designed, sustainable residential and commercial developments, and to be the trusted partner of global citizens in realizing their goals of owning a home or an investment property in the Philippines.

The Milano Residence

The Milano Residences is located in Century City, Makati. Long known as the epicenter of Philippine social, cultural, and financial activity, Makati is constantly evolving and reinvigorating itself – a true megalopolis that holds its own against other major world urban centers.

The Milano Residences stands on some of the most prime and expensive real estate within this modern, future-ready district – a city in constant progress.

Amenities

Piazza

Be transported to Italy as you step into this fabulous Italian style market square. Complete with al fresco cafes and Juice Bar - serving a variety of soft and alcoholic beverages - for the residents to enjoy, it's a fantastic place for a relaxing morning spent enjoying great coffee and panini.

Pool

The colorful pool is always inviting, especially on a warm day. Enjoy a relaxing soak, or swim a few lengths in the morning. Relax on the pool's edge and feel stress slipping away.

Grand Lobby

Whether it's the dramatic lighting or the stylish furniture, your first impression as you enter will be one of the amazement. Each arrival soon becomes a celebration as you enjoy the fantastic design and furnishings. It's a great place to greet guests and sets a real precedent as you head for the elevator.

Gym

No need to head out to get some all important exercise. The well equipped gym comes complete with all the equipment you might need and offers the chance to work out in privacy and comfort.

Spa

The spa is a great place to wind down after a log day.

Services

It doesn't end there either. The property employes extremely well trained staff to help you with everything you need. A number of additional services are offered, allowing you to get on with the things that are important to you.

  • Concierge: On call 24/7, the highly trained concierge offers a number of services to residents, from arranging taxis to booking appointments and reserving amenities.
  • Laundry and Dry Cleaning: for a small fee, have your laundry professionally washed and dried for you. Simply drop off and pick up from the laundry area.
  • Electric and Maintenance Services: On call 24/7, if you need anything fixing or if anything goes wrong, simply get in touch with the concierge and the maintenance team will be with you shortly.
  • Security Highly trained, friendly and vigilant security staff monitor CCTV and patrol the property 24/7
